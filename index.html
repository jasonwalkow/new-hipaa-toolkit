Policies & Procedures

HIPAA Toolkit Policies and Procedures are in compliance with current regulations, including HIPAA's Privacy Rule, Security Rule, Enforcement Rule, and Breach Notification Rule.

Education Portal

Included in the Training Plan, our online education portal has everything you need to get you and your staff trained on HIPAA compliance, including video lessons, self-paced modules, and post-assessments for comprehension.

Monthly Live Webinar

Once you have purchased the Training Plan, you and your staff will be scheduled for a live HIPAA Compliance Webinar from a Certified Healthcare Compliance (CHC) Consultant monthly for one year.

Training Materials

In addition to the live webinar included in the Training Plan, you and your staff will receive training materials for new hires and ongoing education. Training materials are easy-to-follow and aesthetically modern.

Monthly Newsletter

As a part of our Monthly HIPAA Subscription, read the latest breaking HIPAA news from our newsletter and stay up-to-date on recent enforcement actions and guidance announcements.

Blog Access

As part of our Monthly HIPAA Subscription, learn about the latest HIPAA events and happenings from industry leaders. Gain insight into the industry and upcoming education events.

HIPAA Handouts

Our handouts include a HIPAA F.A.Q., one-pagers for Privacy Officers, HIPAA flyers, and other resources to help you promote HIPAA compliance.
